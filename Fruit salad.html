Fruit Salad

Ingridients:

Salad

  • 8 cups cubed seedless watermelon
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 1/2 cups halved strawberries
  • 6–7 large mint leaves, chopped small
  • 4 ounces of feta, crumbled

Drizzle

  • 2 tablespoon fresh lime juice (about 1 whole lime)
  • 3–4 tablespoon honey (if your fruit is super sweet, use less)
  • pinch of sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon tequila *optional

Additional

  • HIGH QUALITY thick Balsamic Vinegar *optional but it’s AMAZING in this salad

Instructions:

  1. For the Honey Lime Drizzle: In a small bowl whisk together the honey, lime, sea salt, and optional tequila until well combined. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl add the watermelon, blueberries, strawberries, feta, and mint. Gently toss to combine.
  3. If serving immediately, pour over the honey-lime glaze and gently toss. Drizzle on some balsamic vinegar and serve. If serving later, put the salad in the fridge WITHOUT the honey-lime drizzle and cover. Right before serving, add the drizzle and balsamic vinegar.
