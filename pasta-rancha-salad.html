Pasta Rancha Salad

Ingridients:

Homemade Buttermilk Ranch

  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup buttermilk ranch seasoning
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Pasta salad

  • 1/2 pound cooked pasta
  • 3 cups cooked chicken, chopped
  • 6 slices thick-cut cooked bacon, crispy and chopped
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar, freshly shredded
  • 3 green onions

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the ranch dressing and set aside. To a large bowl add the pasta salad ingredients and pour in 1/2 -3/4 of the ranch dressing. Gently mix, and coat all of the ingredients.
  2. Transfer to a covered bowl and refrigerate for 1 hour. Right before serving, mix and if necessary, add some of the remaining ranch dressing.
  3. This can be served hot, warm, or cold. Store leftovers in the fridge.
